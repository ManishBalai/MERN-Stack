Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • Cashew nuts (kaju) - 1 cup
  • Sugar - 1/2 cup
  • Water - 1/4 cup
  • Ghee (clarified butter) - 1 tsp
  • Cardamom powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Silver leaf (varak) for decoration

Process

  1. Preparing Cashew Powder
  2. Preparing Sugar Syrup
  3. Making the Dough
  4. Shaping Kaju Katli
  5. Decorating

Ras-gulla

Ingredients

  • For Paneer (Chhena):
    • Milk: 1 liter (full cream preferred)
    • Lemon juice/vinegar: 2-3 tablespoons
  • For Sugar Syrup:
    • Sugar: 1.5 cups
    • Water: 4 cups
    • Cardamom powder: 1/4 teaspoon
    • Rose water: 1 teaspoon

Process

  1. Make the Paneer (Chhena)
  2. Knead the Paneer
  3. Prepare the Sugar Syrup
  4. Cook the Rasgullas
  5. Cool and Serve

Rabri

Ingredients

  • Milk (full-fat): 1 liter
  • Sugar: 3-4 tablespoons (adjust to taste)
  • Cardamom powder: 1/4 teaspoon
  • Saffron strands: A pinch (soaked in 1 tablespoon of warm milk)
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios): 2-3 tablespoons
  • Rose water (optional): 1/2 teaspoon

Process

  1. Boil the Milk
  2. Reduce the Milk
  3. Add Flavorings
  4. Garnish
  5. Cool and Serve

Kalakand

Ingredients

  • Milk (full-fat): 1 liter
  • Condensed milk: 1 cup
  • Cardamom powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios): 2-3 tablespoons
  • Lemon juice or vinegar: 2 tablespoons
  • Ghee: 1 teaspoon (for greasing)

Process

  1. Make Paneer (if not using store-bought)
  2. Prepare the Kalakand Mixture
  3. Add Flavors
  4. Set and Garnish
  5. Cut and Serve
